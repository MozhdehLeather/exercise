🧘‍♂️ Paraspinal Exercises

Follow these simple exercises to stretch and strengthen your spine! 🏋️‍♂️

1️⃣ Stretching the Right Side

🔹 Side Bend Stretch

  • Stand with feet shoulder-width apart 👣
  • Raise your left hand over your head 🙋‍♂️
  • Slowly bend towards the right side ➡️
  • Hold for 15-20 seconds and return to normal ⏳
  • Repeat 3-5 times 🔁

2️⃣ Strengthening the Left Side

🔹 Superman Exercise

  • Lie on your stomach on a soft surface 🛏️
  • Stretch your hands forward ✋
  • Slowly lift your left arm and right leg at the same time ⬆️
  • Hold for 5 seconds, then relax ⏳
  • Repeat 10 times 🔁

3️⃣ General Spinal Stretch

🔹 Cat-Cow Stretch

  • Get on all fours 🐱
  • Cow Pose: Look up, arch your back downwards 🐄
  • Cat Pose: Look down, round your back upwards 🐈
  • Repeat 10 times 🔁

✅ Additional Tips

  • Do exercises daily for best results 📅
  • Go slow and steady, don’t force movements 🐢
  • Maintain good posture throughout the day 🏋️
  • Follow up with the doctor regularly 🩺
